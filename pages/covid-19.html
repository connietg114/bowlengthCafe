NOAH’S ARK DURING COVID-19

LAST UPDATED 12/08/2020

WE KEEP THIS PAGE UP-TO-DATE WITH THE LATEST NEWS ABOUT WHAT WE DO DURING THE COVID-19 CRISIS. THANK YOU FOR YOUR SUPPORT AND HELP, DON’T FORGET TO STAY SAFE WHILE STAYING BUBBLY!


Dear Customers,

As per the latest Government regulations and alert levels, we are required to bring some safety measures back to the stores, and we are committed to making necessary modifications during this time.

UPDATE FOR LEVEL 3

At Alert Level 3, our Auckland store will be closed to comply with the shopping centre restriction. However, it will be open under Alert Level 2.


UPDATE FOR LEVEL 2

While Wellington is now on alert level 2, please help us to maintain a safe environment by making sure either scan the check-in QR code or sign in to record your visit at the store. To follow the alert level restriction, limit amount of people entering the store will apply, as well as the social distancing.

LowerHutt Store services will be available by front door ONLY, as the store often gets hard to manage at a busy period.

Our main priority during this time is to ensure the health and safety of our amazing community of customers and staff, and we will continue to act with this in mind until we are sure that enjoying Noah’s Ark bubble tea is a risk-free experience. Our team will be sanitising their hands and surfaces as often as possible while serving customers. We will update you on any and all changes to Noah’s Ark as they happen, and we are grateful for your support and cooperation through this trying time. We could not ask for a better community to surround ourselves with and hope you all stay safe and stay bubbly.


The Noah’s Ark Team



DELIVERY / UBEREATS

Food delivery apps are going to be a great way to ensure you can enjoy quality cuisine at home, so we will be jumping back onto UberEats. Our UberEats menu currently only displays our top 20 as we found the interface couldn’t represent our full range in a comprehensive way. However, if you are craving your old favourite then just leave a note on your order and we’ll make anything you like.

Our menu on UberEats is a bit pricey due to the large surcharge the system imposes. As much as we’d love to take the hit and cut prices, it’s not something we’re able to facilitate at the moment.

While UberEats does have pick up option, we really encourage and recommend customers to order through Phone Call order if you wish to pick up. So you don’t have to pay extra for UberEats services.


PICK UP (PHONE CALL + UBEREATS)

(REGULR APP SERVICES HAS BEEN PAUSED)

Since Alert Level 3 we have been available on the Regulr online ordering app. This has been an incredibly useful tool for minimising contact over this period, and we think the app itself shows great promise.

However, we have received a large amount of feedback from customers have had issues with the app and payment, and so do we. Therefore, we have decided to pause the services on Regulr as the life is now getting back to normal. If you wish to do a pick up order please call us to make an order.


SUPPORTING LOCAL

New Zealanders have come together to fight the COVID crisis, but we ask you to continue the effort by buying from New Zealand-based businesses. Our economy has suffered for the sake of public help but buying local helps strengthen our community. Noah’s Ark employs 24 awesome Kiwis, providing bubble tea to thousands of equally awesome Kiwis. We’re one of many businesses that put money back into this country’s economy, hit hard by the crisis.

The amazing folks at Gazley Motors began The Gazley Support Local Project to promote Wellington-based businesses, showing where you can buy from to help our community. We would heartily encourage you to give these and other local businesses your support. Wellington is such an amazing city with all its quirky little corners and these organisations help build that brilliant culture. We’ll be promoting this project over the coming months, hoping to help as many people in our community as possible.
